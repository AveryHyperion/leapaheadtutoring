Holiday Workshops

Is your child bored or struggling in Maths and English? Does your child need a boost in Maths and English confidence? We offer different programs and workshops every term. Stay tuned for our holiday workshops in every term.

Workshops Offered:

  • Happy Speller
  • A-Maze-ing Grammar Booster
  • Magic of Multiplications
  • Maths Ninjas
  • 4 Maths Operations
  • Year 5, Year 7 and Year 9 Test Preparation
  • P.E.A.C (Primary Extension and Challenge) Statewide Test
  • GATE (Gifted and Talented) Academic Selective Entrance Test
  • AMC (Australian Mathematics Competition)
  • ICAS (International Competitions and Assessments for Schools)
  • ATAR (Australian Tertiary Admission Rank)

These workshops aim to boost kids’ confidence + shape up with unique rules and techniques to encode and bring joy into the world of Maths and English.
