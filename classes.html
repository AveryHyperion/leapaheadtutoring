Classes

Our after school programs are designed to provide students with additional support and enrichment after their regular school hours. The program includes:

  • Homework Assistance: Help with completing and understanding homework assignments.
  • Interactive Learning: Engaging activities that reinforce classroom learning and foster new skills.
  • Social Development: Activities that promote teamwork, communication, and social skills.

After School, Saturday, and School Holiday Tutoring

We offer tutoring sessions in both English and Maths at an affordable rate. Sessions are available after school, on Saturdays, and during school holidays. Each student receives individualized materials tailored to their learning needs.

Whether your child is struggling, keeping up, or looking for acceleration, our classes welcome all students. The program provides an opportunity for every learner to advance in a supportive and enriching environment.

Pre-kindy Readiness Program

Our preschool program is designed for children aged 4-5 years old. We offer daily or weekly lesson plans that focus on early literacy, Maths, and kindy-prep skills. Perfect for parents looking to give their children a head start in Maths and English before formal schooling begins.

Children are assessed upon enrollment to evaluate their skills and knowledge before starting our program, ensuring they receive the appropriate level of instruction to excel.
