Classes

We run classes through the academic year. All classes are taught in a small class setting (1-3), allowing students to interact and learn from each other while still gaining personal attention from the tutor.

After School, Saturday, and School Holiday Tutoring

We are offering tutoring sessions at an affordable rate in both, English and Maths after school, Saturday and during school holidays. We design materials to meet individual learning needs for each students. We welcome all students whether you are a struggling learner or on track with peers or looking for acceleration to join our classes.

Pre-kindy Readiness Program

early literacy skills, Maths and kindy-prep skills. Perfect for parents who want to give their children a head start with Maths and English before they begin formal education. Children are assessed upon enrolment to gauge their skill level and knowledge upon entering our program.
