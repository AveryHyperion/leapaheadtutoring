Does Leap Ahead Tutoring help?

Yes, Leap Ahead Tutoring has a proven track record of improving students' test scores and results. Our teaching methods and materials are designed to enhance mathematical thinking, problem-solving, and language skills. We have seen significant growth in both enrollment and customer satisfaction.

Does Leap Ahead Tutoring align with the school curriculum?

Absolutely. Our materials are aligned with the Australian Curriculum to cater to students from primary to senior high school. We ensure that students master each concept before moving on to the next, ensuring a solid understanding of the curriculum.

What types of child is Leap Ahead Tutoring for?

Leap Ahead is designed for children of all abilities. Our program helps build a strong educational foundation, with tailored study loads and pacing suited to each child. We ensure mastery of each topic before advancing, helping children tackle challenges with confidence and fostering a desire to learn beyond their current grade level.

Do you have a replacement lesson if my child was absent?

Yes, we offer replacement classes for students who miss a session due to medical conditions or unforeseen circumstances. We aim to ensure that no student falls behind due to missed classes.
