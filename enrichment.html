Enrichment Programs

Holiday Genius Hour

Are you stuck for ideas on how to keep your children entertained and occupied on Sundays? Look no further! Our Holiday Genius Hour offers an award-winning range of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) development fun games every Sunday.

Whether you're looking for educational activities to keep your kids entertained during school holidays or you need some time to yourself for family, shopping, or relaxation, we can meet your needs. Parents are welcome to drop off their children and enjoy some personal time, knowing their kids are engaged and having fun.

AWARD-WINNING GAMES FEATURE:

  • Exciting mix of education and entertainment for children aged 4 and up
  • Ranging from engineering, circuits, gravity, math, to coding
  • Single player to multi-player options
  • Multi-level logic games from very easy to extremely difficult
  • Multiple challenges, from 12 to 120, increasing in complexity from beginner to junior, expert, and master levels

Our mission is to inspire children with 21st-century thinking skills. We strive to foster an engaging, inclusive, and vibrant learning environment that is fun for all kids, regardless of their age or skill level.

Join us for a fun and educational experience that will leave your children excited to come back every week!
