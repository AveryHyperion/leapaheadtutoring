Enrichment Programs

Holiday Genius Hour

Are you stuck for ideas on Sunday to keep your children entertained and occupied? Look no further! We are running award winning STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) development fun games on every Sunday.

Welcome all parents whether you are after educational activities to keep kids entertained or fully occupied with works during school holiday or need a break without kids, we can meet your needs! Parents can drop the kids off and have some time with their loved ones/ family members/ shopping for groceries on Sunday.

AWARD WINNING GAMES feature:

  • Exciting mix of education and entertainment for children aged 4 and up
  • Ranging from engineering, circuits, gravity, math to coding
  • Single player to multi-player
  • Multi-level logic games from very easy to extremely difficult
  • Multiple challenges from 12-120 challenges, increasing in complexity from beginner to junior, then expert to master

Our mission is to inspire children in 21st-century thinking skills. We strive to foster an engaging, inclusive, and vibrant learning environment that is fun for kids of all ages.
